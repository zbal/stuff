Dealing with servers doesn't have to be a pain

Git-powered

Use the Git workflows you know work for your code to manage your servers: fork an entire continuous integration setup, git add a new box on Digital Ocean or accept pull requests for security patches.

Collaborative ops

Invite collaborators to your repositories and start collectively manage your infrastructure. No more wondering how your code gets pushed in production or who can help with adding a Ruby dependency on the dev server.

Best practices included

We take care of tedious tasks and make sure your servers are setup following industry best practices. Don't waste your time setting up iptables or deploying the SSH keys of your team on your servers.

Automate all the things

We make automating your infrastructure downright easy; describe in a few lines your entire build and deployment pipelines and trigger them every time your code is updated on GitHub.

Keep an eye on any change

Your whole infrastructure is in Git; see who does what and when at any moment on any of your machines, may this be a change of configuration on NGINX or a failed deployment.

Snapshot your servers as VM

Need to do some work on your local? Don't waste your time setting up you local stack; at any time you can download a replica of any server as a virtual machine to work on your local.
